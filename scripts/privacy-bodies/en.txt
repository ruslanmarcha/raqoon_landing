Raqoon VPN Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026
Service owner: Qat Bilişim ve Yazılım Teknolojileri Ltd. Şti. (Ankara, Türkiye)
Privacy contact: private@raqoon.app

1. Summary

Raqoon VPN is designed to protect your connection and improve privacy when you use the internet. The app does not collect or inspect browsing activity, DNS queries, or VPN traffic contents.

To operate the service we process only limited technical information, including:
- a randomly generated anonymous device identifier;
- a randomly generated anonymous client identifier;
- app store region information (Apple App Store, Google Play, or Huawei AppGallery);
- confirmation that you are at least 18 years old;
- basic technical diagnostics related to connection stability;
- subscription status and traffic volume needed for your plan limits.

We do not require your name, phone number, or email to use the VPN. We do not use VPN data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or user tracking. We do not sell user data to advertising networks.

2. What our No-Logs policy means

We do not log or store:
- history of visited websites and URLs;
- search queries;
- DNS queries;
- traffic content, including messages, emails, files, and other transmitted data;
- history of your network activity;
- your source IP address as part of an activity history.

We do not link your network activity to your account, device, or payment data for profiling.

Some technical data may be processed temporarily by network equipment while a VPN connection is being established or maintained. This is necessary to carry encrypted traffic and run the service. We do not use it to build browsing history, analyze traffic content, or profile users.

3. Data we collect in the app

3.1. Identifiers and eligibility
- randomly generated anonymous device identifier (to authorize your device and enforce device limits);
- randomly generated anonymous client identifier (to manage the client session);
- app store region (Apple App Store, Google Play, or Huawei AppGallery) (to apply regional availability and store rules);
- confirmation that you are 18 years of age or older.

3.2. Service operation and diagnostics
- subscription status and plan limits;
- volume of data transferred (to enforce your plan, not to see what you accessed);
- time of last connection and connection status;
- basic technical diagnostics related to connection stability and errors (without browsing history, DNS queries, or traffic content).

3.3. Subscriptions and app stores
If you subscribe through the Apple App Store, Google Play, or Huawei AppGallery, the store processes payment. We receive only limited technical data needed to validate subscription status (for example, receipt or token data). We do not store full payment card details.

3.4. Support (only if you contact us)
If you write to support or use the support portal, we process contact details and the content you voluntarily provide. This is separate from VPN session logging.

4. Purposes of processing

We use the data above solely to:
- operate and maintain the VPN service;
- apply regional availability restrictions;
- manage subscriptions and device access;
- improve connection reliability and troubleshoot technical issues;
- ensure compliance with age-related service requirements.

5. Where data is processed (infrastructure)

Our infrastructure is distributed as follows:

United States:
- primary backend systems (account identifiers, subscription data, app store region (Apple App Store, Google Play, or Huawei AppGallery), age confirmation, connection diagnostics described in this Policy);
- customer support portal servers (data you submit when you contact support).

Switzerland:
- load balancing for VPN connections;
- VPN servers that provide your encrypted VPN connection.

Encrypted VPN traffic passes through our Switzerland-based VPN and load-balancing infrastructure. We do not keep logs of websites you visit, DNS queries, or traffic contents.

Limited technical service data may be transferred between the United States and Switzerland only as needed to operate the service, using appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.

6. Third parties

We do not share VPN traffic, browsing activity, DNS queries, or traffic contents with third parties for analytics, advertising, profiling, or tracking.

App store operators (Apple, Google, Huawei) process purchases under their own terms and privacy policies. We receive only the technical subscription validation data described above.

Infrastructure providers host servers and networks; we do not provide them with user activity logs, browsing history, DNS queries, or traffic content. They technically participate in carrying encrypted traffic as required for a VPN to function.

7. No tracking in the Raqoon app

Our mobile apps do not use third-party marketing or product analytics SDKs to track VPN usage. Crash or diagnostic reports may be processed by Apple, Google, or Huawei under platform rules and your device settings; such reports are not used to analyze VPN browsing activity or traffic content.

8. Website (raqoon.app), cookies, and analytics

On raqoon.app we may use cookies and similar technologies for basic site operation, preferences, and web analytics.

The site may use Google Analytics to understand overall traffic and page performance. Website analytics is separate from the VPN app and is not used to analyze VPN traffic, DNS queries, or browsing history inside a VPN session.

If a cookie consent banner is shown, you may accept or reject non-essential cookies.

9. Retention and deletion

Technical data is kept while your subscription is active and as needed to operate the service. If you do not renew, account data may be deleted after a period of inactivity.

You can delete your account and related data in the Raqoon app settings. After deletion we stop providing access and delete or anonymize data that is no longer required.

Some records may be retained for a limited time where required by law, billing verification, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

10. Government and authority requests

Qat Bilişim ve Yazılım Teknolojileri Ltd. Şti. is registered in Türkiye. If we receive a lawful request from a competent authority, we assess it under applicable law.

Because we do not keep activity logs, we cannot provide browsing history, DNS queries, or traffic content. We may only be able to confirm limited information such as account existence, subscription status, or the technical data described in this Policy.

11. Age requirement

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18. We use the in-app age confirmation to enforce this requirement.

12. Security

We apply technical and organizational measures including access controls, encryption in transit, and data minimization.

13. Your rights

You may request information about data linked to your account, delete your account in the app, contact us about privacy, and reject non-essential cookies on the website where available.

Privacy: private@raqoon.app
